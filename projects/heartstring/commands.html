Commands

When a command has either <> or [] in the string, they are fields. [] is an optional field, and <> is a required field.
Don't include these in the command.

General

help: Shows the help menu
ping: Shows the latency and API latency of the bot
say: Makes the bot say whatever you put after the command.
Requires the Manage Messages permission. Usage: h.say <text to say>
uptime: Shows the uptime of the bot in Days, Hours and Minutes
avatar: Shows your avatar, unless prompted with a mention.
Usage: h.avatar [@user]
contact: Contact the developer
Usage: h.contact <reason for contacting>

Moderation

kick: Kicks a user.
Requires the 'Kick' permission Usage: h.kick <@user> [reason]
ban: Bans a user.
Requires the 'Ban' permission Usage: h.ban <@user> [reason]
purge: Deletes the number of messages you specify (between 2 and 100)
Requires the 'Manage Messages' permission Usage: h.purge <amount 2-100>
addrole: Adds the specified role to the specified user.
Usage: h.addrole <user> <role>
removerole: Removes the specified role from the specified user.
Usage: h.removerole <user> <role>

Reddit

meme: Posts a random meme from reddit
reddit: Posts a random post from a subreddit you specify.
Currently only works with images. Usage: h.reddit <subreddit>
dog: Posts a random picture of a dog from r/rarepuppers
cat: Posts a random picture of a cat from r/kittens

Fun

8ball: Acts like a magic 8ball
roll: Rolls a dice. Usage: h.roll <number>
bean: Beans someone, a fake ban.
Usage: h.bean <user> <reason>

Info

userinfo: Shows your user info if not prompted with a mention,
otherwise displays info of the user you have mentioned.
Alias: ui. Usage: h.userinfo [@user]
serverinfo: Shows info on the server
Alias: si
botinfo: h.botinfo: Shows info on the bot
Alias: bi
